My Time In Australia

In 2025 I was lucky enough to go to Australia for 7 weeks. Its been a dream of mine to always go and due to my uncle going there for his sabbatical and saving my holiday time from work I was able to go for such a long period. I spent my time there in Brisbane, Queensland and I was there between February and April which was partly during their summer.

What did I see?

In terms of wild life I came across various animals I’ve never seen before such as....

  1. Koalas
  2. Kangaroos
  3. Dingos
  4. Wombats
  5. Tasmanian Devils

While there are other species of lizards, spiders and snakes that I’ve also came across those were the animals worth noting.

Where did I travel?

Outside of Brisbane I spent some time in Sunshine Coast and Gold Coast within Queensland and later travelled to Sydney in New South Wales.

Would I ever go again?

Hopefully in a few years I can revisit the country and fully travel across its vast land, next time by car.
